Positivity metric calculator

Course Project for Developing Data Products class

Alexey Ermakov

What is it?

  • This is attempt to get emotional context for given text
  • Is it something positive or negative?
  • The app is trying to estimate this and give numeric result on scale 0 to 10
  • 0 means text is totaly negative and you probably shouldn't read it :)
  • 10 means the text is awesome

How it works

  • The team from University of Vermon made a great research Human language reveals a universal positivity bias. They get around 10000 most frequent english words and ask thousands of native speakers to rate each word on a 9 point scale, with 1 corresponding to most negative or saddest, 5 to neutral, and 9 to most positive or happiest.
  • The results were published. For each language they provide 2 files: one with words and one with average rate for each word.
  • Shiny application process given text using Text Mining package removing punctuation, stop words, white spaces and gets matrix of words and their counts.
  • Then using files from the research app is calculating average rate of the dictionary words which were in text.
  • The result is normalized number from 0 to 10 which represents "positivity" of given text and 2 lists with most positive and most negative words

get matrix of words for text from the news
